✦   Who I Am

About Me

Namaskaram! I am Harshitha Kumaresan, a dedicated Bharathanatyam artist trained in the classical traditions of Tamil Nadu. My passion for this sacred dance form has taken me to stages across the state, earning recognition from the Tamil Nadu Government and the global Guinness World Record community.

"Every mudra is a prayer, every footstep an offering — Bharathanatyam is not just a dance, it is the very language of the divine."

I hold an Artist's ID Card issued by the Tamil Nadu Government and have been honoured with the prestigious Kalai Ilamani Award. I am also a proud participant in the Guinness World Record at Chidambaram. Currently, I am advancing my expertise through a Master's degree in Bharathanatyam at Annamalai University.
